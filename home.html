Adventure Base Camp
Kithulgala


Adventure base camp Kithulgala give you the best because nature lovers deserve the best. there are A/C and non A/C rooms, FAmily rooms and single rooms, Dometry, Cabana and Tents which you can choose by your preferences.

Rooms are consists with soothing interior decor with attached bathrooms. What's more with a balcony attached to each room, and you can enjoy the amazing scenic beauty of Kelani river and mountains which extends beyond the balcony.

Meals at the adventure base camp Kithulgala are an electric mix of both eastern and western cuisine. There are two resturants setting facing the natural pool, Kelani river and the mountains giving you a sense of peace and relaaxation.
